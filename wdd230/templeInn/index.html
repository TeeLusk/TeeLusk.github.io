Who We Are

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company

Our goal is to provide quality service so you can focus on the important things

What We Do

We specialize in meeting needs of temple patrons and visitors as they serve in temples throughout the world, families participating in sealings, as well as wards and youth groups. Listed below are some of the speicialzed services we offer:

  • Free changing rooms, bathroom facilities
  • Low cost Playroom
  • Themed reception and sitting areas

Featured Temple: Idaho Falls, Idaho

The Idaho Falls Temple is one of many of our temples included in our hotel network. Recently rededicated in 2017, the Idaho Falls temple provides a beatiful backdrop to those walking the Greenbelt around the adjacent Snake River.

Please note with the current world situation this temple is only open for living sealings only, for those who have been previously endowed. More information can be found on our temples page or the Church's temple page.
