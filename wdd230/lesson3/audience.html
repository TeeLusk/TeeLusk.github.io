Target Audience

Target audience for this will be the average user looking for a quick glimpse at any upcoming inclement weather.


Personas

Sheldon Dinkleberg

Gary is a general contractor who needs to keep work going at all of his job sites. Because Gary is so busy managing projects, he doesn't have time to take a deep dive into the weather and needs information fast

Clarence Dinkleberg

Clarence is an avid gardener who isn't able to build a greenhouse so she is very concerned about any upcoming weather that would possibly harm her dear plants.


Scenarios

  • Do I need to bring a coat/rainjacket?
  • (Similar) What kind of clothes should I wear today?
  • Will it rain/snow today?
  • Do I need to cover my plants overnight?
  • How long is the inclement weather supposed to last?
